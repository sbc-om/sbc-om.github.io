SBC Roadmap

Our History and Vision for the Future

  • 06/03/2022
    Company Foundation
    Senior Blockchain Company (SBC) was born with a vision to revolutionize industries by leveraging blockchain technology. Our mission is to provide transparency, efficiency, and trust through custom blockchain-based solutions tailored to businesses of all sizes. From the outset, SBC focused on creating an ecosystem for decentralized innovations across various domains, including finance, healthcare, supply chain, and real estate.
  • 2023
    Launch of Blockchain Platform
    SBC successfully developed and launched its dedicated blockchain platform, enabling businesses to securely manage transactions, data, and operations. This platform provided the foundation for building decentralized applications (DApps) and enterprise solutions tailored to client needs.
  • 2024
    DeFi and Web3 Platforms
    SBC expanded its offerings by launching advanced **DeFi platforms** and **Web3 solutions**, empowering businesses and users with decentralized financial tools and transparent operations. Current projects include: - Decentralized lending and borrowing platforms. - Tokenized business models for revenue sharing. - Web3-powered digital identity and data ownership solutions.
  • October 2024
    SBC Token Launch on TON
    SBC launched its native token on the TON network, marking a major milestone in its journey. The token acts as the backbone of the SBC ecosystem, enabling seamless integration with DeFi, crowdfunding, and tokenized assets. This launch reflects our commitment to creating a decentralized financial ecosystem accessible to all.
  • 2024
    Crowdfunding Solutions
    We launched a cutting-edge **crowdfunding platform**, enabling startups and entrepreneurs to raise capital through blockchain-backed solutions. This platform facilitates trustless funding processes, ensuring transparency and accountability while connecting investors directly with projects they believe in.
  • 2025
    Preparing for Global Exchange Listings
    SBC is building the infrastructure necessary for global exchange listings. This includes meeting the compliance, security, and liquidity requirements of top-tier exchanges. Our goal is to ensure the SBC token achieves widespread recognition and adoption.
  • 2026-2027
    Tokenization of Profitable Businesses
    SBC will expand its tokenization services to include profitable businesses across various sectors. This initiative will allow investors to participate in revenue-generating businesses through blockchain, opening new avenues for growth and democratizing access to investment opportunities.
  • 2028
    Real Estate Tokenization
    By 2028, SBC aims to transform the real estate market by tokenizing properties. This initiative will allow fractional ownership, simplified transactions, and secure investments in real estate assets, breaking traditional barriers to property ownership.
  • 2030 and Beyond
    Driving Future Blockchain Innovations
    SBC will continue pushing the boundaries of blockchain, DeFi, and Web3. Our focus will remain on developing state-of-the-art solutions for emerging challenges, ensuring we remain at the forefront of blockchain innovation.
